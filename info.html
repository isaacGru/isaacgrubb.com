Isaac Grubb
Bio Behind the Scenes Showreel

I am a visual artist whose practice explores the intricacies of the human condition and human experience. With curating experience I am passionate about bringing art forms together to nurture and ignite artistic visions. As a freelance technician I ensure high quality of detail to deliver cutting edge digital and physical exhibitions. I am a creatively versatile individual who strives to get the best possible outcome in all situations with the desire to constantly learn and experiment with new techniques and technologies. I am hardworking, open-minded and solutions-oriented with an enthusiasm for digital cultures and collaboration.
